🔧 Twilio Setup — 5 minutes
Twilio gives you a real phone number. Texts you send to it will trigger Meridian to respond with your PM context.
1
Create a Twilio account
Go to twilio.com and sign up for free. You get a $15 trial credit — enough for thousands of SMS messages.
2
Get a phone number
In the Twilio console, go to Phone Numbers → Buy a number. Pick any US number with SMS capability (~$1/month).
3
Set your webhook
In your number's settings, set the Messaging webhook to your Meridian backend URL: https://yourapp.com/api/sms
4
Add your credentials here
Paste your TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID, TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN, and phone number below. Meridian will handle the rest.
5
Text your number
Save the Twilio number in your phone as "Meridian". Try texting: "What's my day look like?" or "Send my standup to Slack"
